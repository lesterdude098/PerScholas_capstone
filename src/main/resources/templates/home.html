ROJBUILDS

My name is Roger Abangan. I am 51 years old and I live in Owings Mills with my loving wife, Bernadeth, together with our kids- Lester, Pauline, Jericho and Paolo.

When I am not working on building my next project, I enjoy watching movies and playing music. I play the guitar, bass, drums and piano. Some of my favorite musicians are The Beatles, Van Halen and Steve Vai.

I grew up in Cebu City Philippines and obtained a Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering from University of San Jose Recoletos. I worked as a gunsmith for several years, before moving to the United States, working as a machine operator. I recently quit my full time job to build custom furniture and do home improvement projects full time. Thank you for your support!
